Did you know?

Since it's fall now we thought we'd share some interesting words relating to fall in Japan.

  • 月見 (つきみ, tsukimi) - Moon viewing
  • 秋祭り (あきまつり, aki matsuri) - Fall festival
  • 焼き栗 (やきぐり, yaki guri) - Roasted chestnuts
  • ハロウィーン - Halloween
  • 紅葉 (こうよう, kouyou) - Autumn colors

Tips for learing


Textbooks

Textbooks are a great resource to learn Japanese. Whether your learning in a classroom or learning by yourself, there are plenty of great textbooks out there. Check out our Resources Page for more information.

Immersion

Fully immersion is probably the best way to learn a language. This may be difficult if you're not living in Japan. However, reading books, watching TV shows and practicing speaking with others are great for learning the language.

Have Fun

No matter what your reason for learning the language is, it's important to enjoy youself and have fun. Everyone makes mistakes and that's important to the learning process. Meet others learning the language through our Connect Page.
